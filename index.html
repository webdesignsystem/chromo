Introduction  to NCBI

With 18.2 million cases of COVID-19 virus, in the United States, ncbi.nlm.nih.gov is the second most visited .gov domain while scientists search for a cure.

Founded in 1988

National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI) was founded in 1988.

10+ Databases

NCBI is a resource for genomic, genetic, and biomedical data that fight human diseases.

127,111,284 Visitors

ncbi.nlm.nih.gov has had a total of 127,111,284 visitors over the last 30 days.

A Reusable  System for Scale

Chromo is a scalable design system that’s accessible and mobile-friendly for NCBI’s government website dedicated to science for the public.

The Basics that Create Consistency

Chromo’s foundational basics that make scaling effortless.

Color

Use of a color palette intentionally, consistently, and sensitively for science.

Icons

Font Awesome icons are a open source, screen-reader friendly web font.

Spacing

Based on multiples of 8px for small, medium, and larger devices.

Buttons

Buttons to draw attention to important actions in light and dark theme.

Font

Public Sans, a strong neutral typeface designed for legibility.

Grid

Flexible mobile-first grid, by flexbox, based on 12 columns.

Accessibility

Focus state for users.
